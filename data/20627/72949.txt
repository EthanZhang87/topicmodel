The millimetre international spelling SI unit symbol mm or millimeter American spelling is a unit of length in the International System of Units SI equal to one thousandth of a metre which is the SI base unit of length Therefore there are one thousand millimetres in a metre There are ten millimetres in a centimetre One millimetre is equal to micrometres or nanometres Since an inch is officially defined as exactly millimetres a millimetre is equal to exactly of an inch Definition Since the metre has been defined as the length of the path travelled by light in vacuum during a time interval of
