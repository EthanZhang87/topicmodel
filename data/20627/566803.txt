title: Hectometre
id: 566803
The hectometre International spelling as used by the International Bureau of Weights and Measures SI symbol hm or hectometer American spelling is a unit of length in the International System of Units SI equal to one hundred metres and to one tenth of a kilometre The word comes from a combination of metre and the SI prefix hecto meaning hundred It is not commonly used in English A football field either soccer or American is approximately hectometre in length The hectare ha a common metric unit for land area is equal to one square hectometre hm See also Orders of magnitude length Conversion of units References External links The dictionary definition of hectometre at Wiktionary
